## 1. The Resource Curse: Oil Addiction
*   **The Setup:** Venezuela sat on the world's largest oil reserves. They thought they were rich forever.
*   **The Mistake:** They focused only on oil and let farms and factories die.
*   **India:** We don't have oil, but we have a "Service Sector" addiction (IT). We neglected Manufacturing (Phase 6). If AI kills IT, we are vulnerable.

## 2. Price Controls: Killing the Market
*   **The Policy:** Government fixed prices of bread and milk to help the poor.
*   **The Result:** Farmers stopped producing because they lost money. Shops went empty.
*   **India:** We use "Export Bans" and "Price Caps" on onions/wheat (Phase 8). It hurts farmers and kills long-term supply.

## 3. Nationalization: State Control
*   **The Action:** The Govt seized companies to run them "for the people."
*   **The Outcome:** Mismanagement and corruption destroyed those companies.
*   **India:** We have a history of "PSU Inefficiency" (Phase 8). The state should regulate, not run businesses.

## 4. Hyperinflation: Printing Poverty
*   **The Cause:** To pay for welfare, they printed money.
*   **The Reality:** Money became worthless. People needed a wheelbarrow of cash to buy bread.
*   **India:** We are fiscally prudent (Phase 33), but high inflation in food/medical (Phase 12) erodes the poor's savings.

## 5. Welfare as a Weapon: Buying Loyalty
*   **The Strategy:** Massive handouts to keep the poor dependent on the party.
*   **The Trap:** The poor stayed poor and dependent.
*   **India:** 800 million people on free food rations. Is it a safety net or a political tool?

## 6. Brain Drain: Losing the Builders
*   **The Exodus:** Millions of Venezuelans fled. Engineers, doctors, teachers left first.
*   **India:** Our HNI and Student exodus (Phase 15) is a slow-motion version of this.

## 7. Institutions: The Erosion
*   **The Shift:** Courts, Army, and Election Commission became loyal to the Leader, not the Constitution.
*   **India:** Concerns about "Institutional Capture" (Phase 8) and Media control (Phase 17) are warning signs.

## 8. Sanctions: The External Hammer
*   **The Impact:** US sanctions crippled them further.
*   **India:** We rely on global trade. If we get isolated (Phase 23), our economy is fragile.

## 9. The Military in Business
*   **The Trend:** Generals were given control of industries.
*   **India:** Our military is professional, but "Crony Capitalism" (Phase 17) plays a similar role of concentrating economic power.

## 10. Synthesis: When Welfare Kills Wealth
1.  **Production First:** You cannot distribute wealth you haven't created.
2.  **Prices Matter:** Let the market set prices, or supply vanishes.
3.  **Institutions Matter:** If you break the courts, you break the economy.
4.  **The Conclusion:** Venezuela shows that a rich country can become a beggar if it chooses **Populism over Economics**.
