## 1. Land Reform: The Catastrophe
*   **The Event:** Zimbabwe seized productive farms from skilled owners and gave them to political cronies.
*   **The Result:** Production collapsed. The "Breadbasket of Africa" became a beggar.
*   **India Parallel:** We have "Fragmented Land." 86% of farmers own tiny plots (<2 acres). They cannot use modern tech. It's not a seizure, but a structural paralysis.

## 2. Hyperinflation: The Trillion Dollar Note
*   **The Action:** Printing money to pay debts.
*   **The Image:** People carrying bags of cash to buy milk.
*   **The Lesson:** Currency value is based on "Trust" and "Production," not paper.

## 3. Cronyism: Economy for the Few
*   **The Reality:** The elite (ZANU-PF) got rich while the country starved.
*   **India Parallel:** The "Billionaire Raj" (Phase 17). Wealth concentration at the top while the bottom struggles.

## 4. Brain Drain: The Exit
*   **The Flight:** Millions of educated Zimbabweans left for South Africa/UK.
*   **India:** Our "Donkey Route" and Student Exodus (Phase 15). When hope leaves, the economy dies.

## 5. Unemployment: The 90%
*   **The Stat:** 90% formal unemployment in Zimbabwe. Everyone works in the "Informal Sector" (street vending).
*   **India:** We are seeing the "Informalization" of jobs (Phase 6). Gig work and street vending are disguising the lack of real jobs.

## 6. Destruction of Assets
*   **The Process:** Farms were ruined, factories looted.
*   **India:** We are not destroying physical assets, but we are eroding "Human Capital" (Phase 2). An uneducated generation is a destroyed asset.

## 7. Political Denial
*   **The Rhetoric:** Mugabe blamed "Western Imperialists" for the failure.
*   **India:** We often blame "Global Headwinds" or "Foreign Soros Conspiracies" instead of fixing internal rot.

## 8. Black Market: The Real Economy
*   **The Survival:** People traded in US Dollars illegally.
*   **India:** We have a massive "Black Economy" (Real Estate/Gold) to evade taxes and regulations.

## 9. Health Collapse
*   **The Crisis:** Cholera outbreaks because water systems failed.
*   **India:** Our Water Crisis (Phase 27) and poor public health (Phase 38) risk similar outbreaks.

## 10. Synthesis: When Production Stops
1.  **Competence Matters:** You need skilled people to run farms/factories.
2.  **Property Rights:** If the state can take your asset arbitrarily, no one invests.
3.  **Denial Kills:** Blaming outsiders doesn't fix the problem.
4.  **The Conclusion:** Zimbabwe is an extreme example of **Institutional Suicide**. India's risks are subtler (slow decay of institutions) but the direction—valuing politics over productivity—is a dangerous echo.
