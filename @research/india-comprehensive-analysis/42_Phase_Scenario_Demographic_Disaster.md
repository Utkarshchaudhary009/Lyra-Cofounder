# Phase 42: Scenario B - The Demographic Disaster
*Theme: Scenario Planning (Future Paths)*

This document outlines the worst-case scenario: When the youth population explodes into chaos.

## 1. The Trigger: Jobless Growth Continues
*   **The Math:** 10 million youth enter the workforce every year. The economy creates only 2 million jobs.
*   **The Backlog:** Every year, the army of "Angry Young Men" grows by 8 million. By 2030, that’s 40-50 million frustrated, military-age men with no work.

## 2. Civil Strife & Unrest
*   **Protests:** Agneepath and Reservation riots were just the trailer.
*   **Scale:** We could see massive, coordinated city shutdowns.
*   **Target:** Anger turns against the "System"—burning trains, buses, and targeting the assets of the rich (Phase 17).

## 3. Crime Surge: The "Purge" Reality
*   **Street Crime:** Snatching, robbery, and kidnapping for ransom spike.
*   **Cyber Crime:** Jamtara-style scams become the primary "industry" for educated unemployed youth.
*   **Safety:** Cities become unsafe zones, forcing the middle class to live in fortress-like societies with private guards.

## 4. Balkanization Risks (State vs. State)
*   **The Spark:** The North-South tax divide (Phase 14) breaks the union's trust.
*   **The Conflict:** Southern states might refuse to send money to the North. Northern states might use their political population power to force laws on the South.
*   **Result:** A paralyzed central government and a fractured nation.

## 5. Global Isolation
*   **Reputation:** Daily riots and internet shutdowns scare away all FDI (Phase 5).
*   **Visa Bans:** Other countries stop giving visas to Indians, fearing illegal immigration (Phase 7). India becomes "Walled In."

## 6. Synthesis: The Nightmare Scenario
1.  **Chaos:** The streets belong to the mob.
2.  **Collapse:** The economy shrinks as businesses close due to violence.
3.  **The Verdict:** The "Dividend" becomes a **Bomb**. The energy of the youth, having no creative outlet (Jobs), finds a destructive outlet (Violence). India becomes a "Failed State" with nuclear weapons.
