# Phase 45: The "Oligarchy" Path (Russia Style)
*Theme: Scenario Planning (Future Paths)*

This document analyzes the scenario where India becomes a playground for a few super-rich families while the rest struggle.

## 1. State Capture
*   **The Definition:** When private corporations are so powerful they effectively "Own" the government.
*   **The Mechanism:** They fund elections (Electoral Bonds), own the media (Phase 40), and write the laws that regulate them.
*   **The Result:** The government works for the Shareholder, not the Citizen.

## 2. Wealth Concentration
*   **The Trend:** The top 1% own 40% of wealth. In this scenario, that goes to 60-70%.
*   **The Landscape:** Small businesses die (Phase 16). Every shop, service, and utility is owned by a franchise of a Big Conglomerate. The "Middle Class Entrepreneur" goes extinct.

## 3. Suppression of Dissent
*   **The Control:** Anyone who questions the Oligarchs is labeled "Anti-National."
*   **The Tool:** Police and agencies (ED/CBI) are used as private security for the Oligarchs to crush competition and critics.

## 4. Resource Extraction
*   **The Focus:** The economy shifts to "Rent Seeking"—extracting coal, operating ports, selling phone data—rather than creating value.
*   **Elite Flight:** The Oligarchs live in London or Dubai (Phase 15), earning money in India but spending it abroad. They have no stake in the quality of Indian schools or hospitals because they don't use them.

## 5. Synthesis: Rich Tycoons, Poor Nation
1.  **Feudalism 2.0:** We return to a system of "Lords" (Oligarchs) and "Serfs" (Gig Workers).
2.  **No Mobility:** A poor child has zero chance of becoming rich because the ladder has been pulled up.
3.  **The Verdict:** India becomes a **Russia-style Oligarchy**. It looks rich from the outside (fancy airports, billionaires in Forbes list), but the inside is hollow and rotting.
