# Phase 50: The Governance Deficit
*Theme: Specific Sectoral Rot*

This document highlights the decline in democratic accountability using 2024 data.

## 1. The "No Debate" Parliament
*   **Real Data (2024):** **89% of the Union Budget** was passed without discussion in the session.
*   **The Trend:** In 2023, 30 bills were passed, many with opposition suspended or absent.
*   **Impact:** Laws are passed without scrutiny, leading to implementation failures later.

## 2. RTI: The Closing Door (3.86% Rejection)
*   **The Data:** RTI rejection rate rose to **3.86%** in 2023-24 (up from 3.21%).
*   **The Meaning:** Government officials are increasingly refusing to share information with citizens.
*   **Corruption Risk:** Opaque governance breeds corruption (Rank 93 in CPI).

## 3. Internet Shutdowns (Rank #2)
*   **The Record:** 84 shutdowns in 2024.
*   **The Policy:** Digital India shuts down more often than almost any other nation.

## 4. Synthesis: The Eroding Foundation
1.  **Centralization:** Decisions are forced from the top (No debate).
2.  **Opacity:** Information is hidden (RTI rejection).
3.  **The Verdict:** The "Software" of democracy is glitching. Without accountability, economic efficiency is impossible.
