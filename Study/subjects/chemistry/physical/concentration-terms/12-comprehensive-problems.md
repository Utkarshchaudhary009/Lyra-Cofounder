## No New Theory. Pure Problem-Solving.

This chapter is your **exam simulator**. Every concept from Chapters 1–11 can appear — individually or combined. Problems are grouped by difficulty, not by topic.

> **How to use this chapter:**
> 1. Don't peek at the answer key until you've genuinely attempted each problem
> 2. Time yourself: Board-level = 2–3 min/problem, JEE-level = 3–5 min/problem
> 3. If stuck for more than 5 minutes, note which concept is blocking you and review that chapter
> 4. After completing a set, identify your weakest type and do 5 more problems of that type from earlier chapters
