## How to Use This Chapter

This chapter is not meant to be "complete." It's a running log of questions that deserve further investigation — either through research, lived experience, or deeper Socratic examination.

**Format:**
- Questions are grouped by theme
- Each question has space for notes, observations, and evolving answers
- Mark questions as `[OPEN]`, `[EXPLORING]`, or `[RESOLVED]` as your understanding develops
