# The Practical Approach to Choosing a Person

> *A living book — built to be questioned, re-evaluated, and grown over time.*

---

## About This Book

This is not a romantic advice book. It is a **framework for clear thinking** about one of the most consequential decisions a human makes — choosing who to share life with.

Every claim here was built by questioning until nothing was left to question. No assumption was accepted without examination. What survived scrutiny is what remains.

**How to use this book:**
- Read sequentially the first time for full context
- Return to individual chapters as life presents specific situations
- Use the [Open Questions](./08-open-questions.md) chapter to add new inquiries as they arise
- Re-evaluate chapters periodically — your understanding will deepen with lived experience

## Core Principles

1. **Question everything.** If a claim doesn't survive scrutiny, discard it.
2. **Observe behavior, not words.** What people DO is who they ARE.
3. **Build yourself first.** You attract what you are, not what you want.
4. **Choose from abundance, not scarcity.** Fear of being alone produces the worst decisions.
5. **Track patterns, not incidents.** A single data point is noise. Ten data points is signal.
